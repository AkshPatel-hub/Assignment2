Biography

Scout, aka Tanmay Singh, is a 27-year-old Indian gaming YouTuber and esports athlete is one of India's most renowned esports personalities and gaming content creators. Born in Valsad, Gujarat, Scout showed a strong interest in sports from an early age, particularly football. He even aspired to become a professional football player and represented his state in national-level tournaments. However, a ligament injury sidelined his dream, leading him to discover gaming as his next passion. Known for his precision and aggressive gameplay,Scout ventured into content creation alongside esports. His YouTube channel, with millions of subscribers, features gameplay videos, vlogs, and entertaining collaborations with other popular creators. His dedication to producing high-quality content has made him a fan favorite. Scout inspires millions worldwide with his motto: "Never Give Up."
